Jerry Hugo


Biographical Information

Jerry Hugo was born on 9/29/19 on the back of a EECS 360 lab instructions packet. During his younger years, Jerry was often made fun of in school for some of his "abnormal" features; mainly hair that looks like squiggly noodles, a stumpy leg, and an eye twice the size of the other. Sticks and stones may break his bones, but words will never hurt Jerry.
